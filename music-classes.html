Music is not just something we do....It is who we are.
“I wish I had learned to play an instrument when I was young.”
This statement is one that many adults make throughout their lives. When most people were young, they did not have an opportunity to learn using cutting-edge technology and brain-based instructional methods. Our innovative, dynamic, social environment captivates and captures the attention of students of all ages.
At Central Creativity, we are 100% focused on growing powerful, independent thinkers and achievers. Statistics clearly show that children who are exposed to musical development for just a short period of time gain 3 IQ points of intellectual advantage. That is quite impressive! Comparatively, students who do not have a background in some form of the Arts are 5 TIMES LESS LIKELY TO GRADUATE HIGH SCHOOL...Five times! If that doesn't speak to the benefits of exposing our youth to the Arts, what can?
We have music classes for all age ranges.
Our Music Program is geared toward developing all learners, regardless of their ability level. We utilize the latest in technological advances and couple that with sound music theory and highly experienced instructors to deliver a vibrant and effective course of instruction. Throughout the year, our students will develop their skillset and their knowledge. The culminating event for all of our music students will be a recital at the end of the year to showcase their achievements and perform in front of an audience.
