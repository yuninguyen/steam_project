Art
Art teaches students to become innovative and creative thinkers. They often transfer the knowledge they have learned in art to increased academic achievement as well. All of our art classes are process-oriented. In other words, we believe the PROCESS is more important than the PRODUCT! Our emphasis is on providing opportunities and experiences that allow children to think critically and creatively. We stress the importance of a child completing a project that they imagined and designed, rather than one that looks “perfect” and was created primarily by the instructor.
Music
With offerings from beginner to advanced, our music classes will strike a chord with novice and experienced musicians of all ages. Divergent instructional strategies and the use of cutting-edge technologies, such as illuminating pianos, make learning to play an instrument more exciting than ever before imagined. Voice classes are sure to hit a high note as we combine traditional technique with innovative, effective teaching strategies for music education. Private lessons in keyboarding, guitar, and voice may be arranged. Additional fees will apply.
S.T.E.A.M.
Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math (S.T.E.A.M.) classes will provide students with opportunities to collaborate and be constructively curious. They will invent, analyze, design, develop, and build their way to meaningful content area connections. These classes, inspired by the “Maker Movement,” are driven by the idea that students learn best by “making.” Challenging and actively engaging students to solve problems through hands-on experimentation and trial and error is the central focus of all these multi-sensory classes.
Culinary Arts
Central Creativity is proud to serve edible education at its finest! Aside from simply learning about food, we bring classroom learning into the kitchen by integrating different academic areas. For example, recipes must be read and labels understood before cooking may begin. Many students will gain a better understanding of math skills such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fractions, geometry and more while whipping up mouth-watering dishes. Science may be incorporated when students create chemical reactions such as freezing, melting, dissolving, and crystalizing that naturally occur in the cooking process.
Literacy
From beginning to advanced readers, all successful students must not only be able to decode and pronounce each word they read, but also understand what they have read. We focus on increasing students reading comprehension and fluency while building their ability to evaluate and analyze what they have read. Write From the Beginning and Beyond®, the writing sequel to Thinking Maps®, will be used to develop and improve students writing skills such as coherence, main ideas and details, organization, punctuation, grammar, and spelling.
Fashion Design
Calling all students with a flair for design and a passion for fashion! Whether students are bringing their visions to life using paper or fabric, they are sure to love expressing their personal design styles by creating unique original projects and one-of-a-kind collections. These classes will teach the fundamentals of illustration and design for fashion and accessories, as well as branding and marketing for the fashion industry. Up-and-coming designers will also learn sewing techniques and how to make fabric selections for their original collections. Finally, by adding some fabulous design challenges and glamorous runway fashion shows, these classes will be all sewn up!
